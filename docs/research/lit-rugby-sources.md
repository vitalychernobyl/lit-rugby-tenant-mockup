# LIT Rugby Research Notes

Research date: 2026-07-07.

## Official LIT7s sources used

- `https://www.lit7s.com/`
  - Page title: LIT 7s 2026 Events.
  - Description references LIT Super 7s Series, 13th annual LIT7s, and 70+ amateur and elite teams from around the world.
  - Public navigation includes LIT7s Tournament, London 7s, LIT Super 7s Series, LIT Rugby Sevens Camp, LIT Florida International 7s, and Contact LIT7s.
  - Public CTA links include Ticketpass team entry/tickets and EventConnect Florida team registration.
- `https://www.lit7s.com/team-registration/`
  - LIT7s registration reference includes GBP 320 entry for a team of 15 players and one manager.
  - Team managers collect tournament pack, tickets, wristbands, match details, and schedule updates.
- `https://www.lit7s.com/sevens-competitions/`
  - Divisions include men's open, men's social, women's open, and women's social.
  - Competition page references 70+ amateur and elite teams, prizes, organization, food, bars, live music, night party, and media.
- `https://www.lit7s.com/prizes-and-awards/`
  - Open winners receive cash prizes and medals.
  - Social winners receive trophies and beer crate prizes.
  - Player of the Tournament awards exist for open and social competitions.
- `https://www.lit7s.com/sevens-rules/`
  - Super Sevens rules page updated April 29, 2026.
  - Summary references up to 14 players, unlimited rolling substitutions, 7 minute halves, and tiebreakers across a multi-tournament series.
- `https://www.lit7s.com/litfloridainternational7s/`
  - LIT Florida International 7s was announced for November 29, 2025 at Premier Sports Campus, Bradenton, Florida.
  - Adult men's, adult women's, U23/university, U18, U16, and possible U14 divisions are referenced.
  - EventConnect registration and hotel links are public.
- `https://www.lit7s.com/the-olympic-experience-lit-rugby-sevens-camp-usa-2026/`
  - LIT Rugby Sevens Camp USA at Olympic Training Center, Chula Vista, California, February 27 to March 1, 2026.
- `https://www.lit7s.com/the-img-experience-lit-rugby-sevens-camp-usa/`
  - IMG Academy camp page references November 14 to 16, 2025 in Bradenton, Florida.
  - Coach listing includes Perry Baker, Naya Tapper, Kelly Griffin, JK Anderson, Irene Gardner, Josh Schnell, and Ellaine Gelman.
